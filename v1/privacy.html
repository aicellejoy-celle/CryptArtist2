Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. The Blank Canvas Guarantee

Welcome to CryptArtist. We believe that your creativity belongs entirely to you. Our core philosophy is absolute privacy. We do not store your generated images, your prompts, or any personal data on our servers.

2. How the App Works (No Data Retention)

When you use our NFT generator:

  • Your prompt is sent securely, via our proxy or directly (if using BYOK), to the OpenAI API for generation.
  • The resulting image is delivered directly to your browser.
  • As soon as the generation is complete, the data vanishes from our serverless functions. We retain absolutely nothing.

3. Bring Your Own Key (BYOK) Mode

If you choose to use your own OpenAI API key to bypass our developer rate limits:

  • Your API key is stored locally in your browser's cookies.
  • It is never transmitted to our servers by the frontend (it only talks directly to OpenAI).
  • You can delete the key from your browser at any time via the settings menu.

4. Rate Limiting Info

For users utilizing our developer key, we track IP addresses temporarily in-memory within our Vercel Serverless Functions to enforce rate limits (1 per minute, 10 per hour). This data is volatile, not written to any database, and disappears automatically.
